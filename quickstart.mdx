### Accessing the Application

Navigate to Xenora https://xenora.ai in your browser to get started with application. 
![Home Page](/doc-images/1.png)

> Have questions? Use the chat in the bottom-right corner anytime.

---

## Authentication & Login
Xenora supports three sign-in methods:
- Google — sign in with your Google account
- Microsoft — sign in with your Microsoft account
- Organization email — enter your work email and we'll send a one-time password (OTP) to verify
___
## Organization Selection
Enter your organization name and accept the Terms of Service to continue.
![Home Page](/doc-images/2.png)

### Select your plan
Choose a plan to continue. Pricing varies by country — see the  Pricing Page [pricing](https://https://preprod.xenora.ai). for details.
Trial and billing

- All paid plans include a 14-day free trial.
- A Free plan is also available if you'd like to try Xenora without a trial period.
- Cancel anytime during the trial — no charges will apply.

Domain selection
- Starter / Free plan — includes one domain.
- Pro plan — includes one domain; additional domains can be added for an extra fee.
- Business plan — includes access to all domains.
