## Authentication & Login

### Screen: Login Page

**Purpose**: Authenticate users and provide secure access to the platform.

#### Available Authentication Methods

Xenora supports OAuth-based authentication:

1. **Sign in with Google**
   - Click the "Sign in with Google" button
   - Select your Google account
   - Grant necessary permissions
   - You'll be redirected to the dashboard

2. **Sign in with Microsoft**
   - Click the "Sign in with Microsoft" button
   - Enter your Microsoft credentials
   - Complete multi-factor authentication (if enabled)
   - You'll be redirected to the dashboard

#### First-Time Sign Up

If you're a new user:

1. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
2. Choose your authentication provider (Google or Microsoft)
3. Complete the OAuth flow
4. Your account will be automatically created
5. You may be prompted to set up your organization (see Section 4)

#### Troubleshooting Login Issues

**Problem**: "Authentication failed" error
- **Solution**: Clear browser cache and cookies, try again
- **Solution**: Ensure pop-ups are enabled for the Xenora domain
- **Solution**: Contact your IT administrator if using SSO

**Problem**: Can't see login buttons
- **Solution**: Disable ad blockers or browser extensions
- **Solution**: Try a different browser

## Organization Selection

### Screen: Tenant Selection

**Purpose**: If you belong to multiple organizations, select which one you want to access.

#### When You See This Screen

- You have accounts with multiple organizations
- You've been invited to join another team
- You're switching between different workspaces

#### How to Select an Organization

1. After logging in, you'll see a list of organizations you have access to
2. Each organization displays:
   - Organization name
   - Your role (Admin, Editor, Read User)
   - Organization logo (if configured)
3. Click on the organization you want to access
4. You'll be redirected to that organization's dashboard
