Xenora is an advanced agentic Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG) platform designed to transform how organizations interact with their knowledge. It empowers teams to centralize information, build intelligent assistants, and deliver conversational AI experiences — internally and for customers.

Built for fast‑moving teams, Xenora integrates with diverse data sources and leverages AI to deliver accurate, context‑aware responses.


## Key Capabilities

### Conversational AI & Chatbots

Create natural, intelligent chat experiences tailored to business use‑cases. Deploy internal assistants or customer‑facing chatbots to streamline communication and support.

### Knowledge Base Insights

Connect Xenora to your organizational knowledge sources to deliver context‑rich, accurate responses. Rely on AI that understands and retrieves highly relevant information.

### Agentic RAG System

Harness Agentic RAG — retrieve, enrich, and generate intelligent responses with advanced retrieval‑augmented pipelines.

### What is Xenora?

Xenora is an AI-powered knowledge management and conversational AI platform designed for modern teams. It enables you to:

- **Centralize Knowledge**: Upload documents, connect cloud storage, and integrate data sources
- **Build AI Assistants**: Create intelligent chatbots powered by your organizational knowledge
- **Deploy Anywhere**: Use internally or embed on external websites
- **Leverage Multiple AI Models**: Support for Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini, DeepSeek, and more
- **Maintain Control**: Enterprise-grade security, role-based access, and complete data ownership

### Key Capabilities

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Conversational AI** | Natural language chat interfaces with context-aware responses |
| **Knowledge Base Management** | Organize and index documents, websites, and cloud data |
| **Agentic RAG System** | Advanced retrieval-augmented generation with multi-step reasoning |
| **External Chatbots** | Embeddable widgets for customer-facing applications |
| **Multi-Model Support** | Choose from 8+ AI providers and models |
| **Team Collaboration** | Role-based access control and team management |
| **Usage Analytics** | Monitor performance, costs, and usage patterns |
