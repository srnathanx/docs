## Custom workflows

Custom workflows are one of Xenora's most powerful and flexible features. They let you design your own document collection processes — tailored exactly to how your business operates.

With custom workflows, you can:

- **Add any number of parties** — collect documents from multiple customers, vendors, or stakeholders in a single case.
- **Define custom document requirements** — specify exactly which documents each party needs to submit.
- **Configure custom fields** — capture structured data alongside the documents.

![Custom Workflow](/doc-images/11.png)
Once created, a custom workflow becomes a reusable template for your team. Run it again for similar cases without rebuilding the process from scratch.

## Using a custom flow

Once created, your custom flow appears under the **Custom Flows** section on the **Flows** page. From there, it works exactly like any built-in flow:

1. Click the custom flow to open it.
2. Create a new case and select the parties involved.
3. Customers receive WhatsApp and email notifications, just like with standard flows.
4. Documents are uploaded, validated, and reviewed through the usual process.
![Custom Workflow](/doc-images/12.png)
There's no separate workflow for cases created from custom flows — your team can use them seamlessly alongside Xenora's built-in flows.
