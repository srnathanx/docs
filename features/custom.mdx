## Custom workflows

Custom workflows are one of Xenora's most powerful and flexible features. They let you design your own document collection processes — tailored exactly to how your business operates.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Multiple parties" icon="users">
    Collect documents from multiple customers, vendors, or stakeholders in a single case.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom requirements" icon="list-check">
    Specify exactly which documents each party needs to submit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom fields" icon="pen-to-square">
    Capture structured data alongside the documents.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Frame caption="Custom workflow builder">
  ![Custom workflow builder](/doc-images/11.png)
</Frame>

Once created, a custom workflow becomes a reusable template for your team. Run it again for similar cases without rebuilding the process from scratch.

## Using a custom flow

Once created, your custom flow appears under the **Custom Flows** section on the **Flows** page. From there, it works exactly like any built-in flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the flow">
    Click the custom flow to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new case">
    Create a new case and select the parties involved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify the customer">
    Customers receive WhatsApp and email notifications, just like with standard flows.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review uploads">
    Documents are uploaded, validated, and reviewed through the usual process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Custom flow case view">
  ![Custom flow case view](/doc-images/12.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  There's no separate workflow for cases created from custom flows — your team can use them seamlessly alongside Xenora's built-in flows.
</Note>
