## Sharing documents with customers

Xenora isn't just for collecting documents — you can also send documents to your customers. Use this when you need to share documents for your customer's reference, or when you need them to review, sign, and send something back.

### How it works

Document sharing uses a built-in flow called **"Share documents with Client"**, available in your practice domain. Each share creates a case so you can track delivery, responses, and completion just like any other case.

### Sharing a document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new case">
    From the **Flows** page, select **Share documents with Client** and click **Create Case**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add customer details">
    Enter the customer's name, email, and mobile number, then create the case.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Share Document tab">
    Inside the case, go to the **Share Document** tab and click **Share Document** to upload the files you want to send.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure response options">
    - Toggle **Response required** if you need the customer to send signed documents back.
    - Use the **Remarks** field to add instructions for the customer (e.g., *"Please sign on page 3 and return"*).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send and track">
    The customer receives the documents via WhatsApp and email. You can track delivery and any returned documents from the case page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

 <Tip>
  You can add multiple documents to a single share — they'll all be sent to the customer together.
</Tip>

### Closing the case

Once the customer has acknowledged the documents (or returned signed copies if required), click **Complete** to close the case.
