<Note>
  The **Teams** feature is available on the **Pro** and **Business** plans.
</Note>

Invite team members to your organization and collaborate on cases together.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Create & assign cases" icon="user-plus">
    Assign new cases to specific team members so ownership is clear from day one.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reassign cases" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Move cases between team members as workloads shift.
  </Card>
  <Card title="View team workload" icon="chart-column">
    See which cases each member is currently handling and rebalance as needed.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

This makes it easy to coordinate document collection across your firm and avoid duplicated effort.

## Adding team members

To add a team member, go to **Settings → Teams → Add User**.

## Roles and permissions

<Info>
  Only **Admin** users can add, disable, or delete team members. Regular users can be assigned to cases but cannot manage team membership.
</Info>
