<Note>
  The **Teams** feature is available on the **Pro** and **Business** plans.
</Note>

Invite team members to your organization and collaborate on cases together. With Teams, you can:

- **Create and assign cases** to specific team members.
- **Reassign cases** between team members as workloads shift.
- **View your team's workload** to see which cases each member is currently handling.

This makes it easy to coordinate document collection across your firm and avoid duplicated effort.

### Adding team members

To add a team member, go to **Settings → Team → Add User**.

### Roles and permissions

Only **Admin** users can add, disable, or delete team members. Regular users can be assigned to cases but cannot manage team membership.
