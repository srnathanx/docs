#### Upgrading Your Plan

**To Upgrade:**

1. Click **"Upgrade"** button on desired plan
2. Confirmation dialog appears:
   - Shows plan comparison
   - Highlights new features you'll get
   - Shows prorated cost (if mid-cycle)
   - Next billing date
3. Review details
4. Click **"Confirm Upgrade"**
5. Enter payment method (if not on file)
6. Upgrade is immediate
7. Success message and confirmation email

**Prorated Billing:**
- If upgrading mid-month, you pay prorated amount
- Example:
  - Free → Starter on day 15 of month
  - Charged: $14.50 (half of $29)
  - Next full charge: $29 on next billing date

**Immediate Access:**
- New limits take effect instantly
- Can create more bots, KBs, etc. right away
- No waiting period

#### Downgrading Your Plan

**To Downgrade:**

1. Contact support (for Starter/Pro)
2. Or click **"Manage Subscription"** → **"Change Plan"**
3. Select lower plan
4. Confirm downgrade
5. **Takes effect at end of current billing cycle**
6. Confirmation email sent

**What Happens During Downgrade:**

**Before End of Cycle:**
- Continue using current plan features
- No immediate changes

**After Billing Cycle Ends:**
- New limits enforced
- If over new limits:
  - Disable excess bots (oldest first)
  - Mark excess KBs as "Archived" (read-only)
  - No new messages if over limit

**Recommendations:**
- Before downgrading, manually reduce usage:
  - Delete unused bots and KBs
  - Export important data
  - Archive old chats
