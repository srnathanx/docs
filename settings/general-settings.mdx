## General Settings

#### Accessing General Settings

1. Click **"Settings"** in left sidebar
2. You'll land on **"General"** tab by default

#### Organization Details

**Organization ID:**
- Unique identifier for your org
- Format: `org_abc123def456`
- **Copy button**: Click to copy to clipboard


**Organization Name:**
- Your company or team name
- Editable by Admins only
- Appears throughout the application

**Tenant ID:**
- Workspace identifier
- Format: `tenant_xyz789`
- **Copy button**: Click to copy
- Used for multi-tenant configurations

**Admin Email:**
- Primary contact email
- Receives important notifications
- Only Admins can edit
- **How to Edit**:
  1. Click the email address
  2. Email field becomes editable (inline)
  3. Type new email
  4. Click **Save** (checkmark icon)
  5. Click **Cancel** (X icon) to discard

#### Advanced Section

**Delete Account** (Admin Only):

**Warning**: This action is irreversible and will permanently delete:
- All knowledge bases and documents
- All chat history
- All external chatbots
- All team members and configurations
- All usage data

**How to Delete:**
1. Click **"Delete Account"** button (red)
2. Confirmation dialog appears
3. Read the warnings carefully
4. Type `DELETE` in the confirmation field (case-sensitive)
5. Click **"Permanently Delete Account"**
6. Account is deleted immediately
7. You're logged out and redirected to login page

**Cannot Delete If:**
- You're not an Admin
- There are other Admins (must transfer ownership first)
- Active subscriptions exist (cancel first)
