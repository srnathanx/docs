## Billing

The **Billing** page shows your current plan and lets you manage your subscription. To access it, click **Billing** in the side menu.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Upgrade your plan" icon="arrow-up-right-dots">
    Move to a higher tier for more users, storage, and domain limits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Buy additional domains" icon="globe">
    Add more domains on the **Pro** plan as your practice grows.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Update payment methods" icon="credit-card">
    Change your card, billing address, or review past charges.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Invoices

The **Invoices** page lists all invoices generated for your organization, including those for monthly subscriptions, annual plans, and any add-ons. You can download individual invoices as PDFs for your records or accounting needs.
