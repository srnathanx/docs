## Billing

The **Billing** page shows your current plan and lets you manage your subscription. Click **Billing** in the side menu to open it.

<CardGroup cols={3}>

  </Card>
  <Card title="Downgrade" icon="arrow-down">

## Invoices

The **Invoices** page lists every invoice generated for your organisation — monthly subscriptions, annual plans, add-on domains, and one-off charges. Download any invoice as a PDF for your records or accounting team.

## Payment methods

The **Payment Methods** page shows the card on file and lets you replace it. Open it from **Billing → Payment Methods**.

You'll be asked to update your payment method when:

- **A charge fails** — Xenora couldn't collect your scheduled payment (expired card, insufficient funds, bank decline).
- **Your card is expiring soon** — A banner appears before the card stops working.
- **You choose to replace it** — Any time, no reason needed.

<Note>
  Replacing the card keeps your existing subscription intact — same plan, same renewal date, no new trial. Only the card on file changes.
</Note>

## Renewal

If a payment fails, Xenora gives you a grace period to fix it before your team loses access. The exact timing depends on your plan status:

| Status | What's happening | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Payment past due** | A scheduled charge didn't go through. Your team still has full access. | Open **Billing → Renew** and complete the payment. Update the card if it's the cause. |
| **Suspended** | Payment didn't recover in time. Your team's access is paused, but your data is intact. | Renew to restore access — nothing is lost. |
| **Archival pending** | Suspension continued past the data-archival threshold. Your data will be archived shortly. | Renew now to keep your account live. Once data is archived, recovery requires support. |

The **Renew** page walks you through any pending payment. It tells you the exact amount due, the consequences if you don't act by a given date, and the next steps after the payment succeeds.

## Add-on domains

On the **Pro** plan, you start with one domain. Need another? Buy an add-on from the Billing page — it's pro-rated to the rest of your current billing period and renews with your subscription.

The **Business** plan includes access to all domains at no extra cost.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription from the Billing page at any time. You'll keep full access until the end of the period you've already paid for; mid-period cancellations aren't pro-rated.

After cancellation, you can also request **deletion of your organisation** — see [Delete organization](/settings/general-settings#delete-organization) for the cool-down and permanent-deletion process.
