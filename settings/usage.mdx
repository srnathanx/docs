## Billing

The **Billing** page shows your current plan and lets you manage your subscription. From here, you can:

- **Upgrade your plan** to access higher user, storage, and domain limits.
- **Buy additional domains** if you're on the **Pro** plan.
- **Update payment methods** and review billing details.

To access billing, click **Billing** in the side menu.

## Invoices

The **Invoices** page lists all invoices generated for your organization, including those for monthly subscriptions, annual plans, and any add-ons. You can download individual invoices as PDFs for your records or accounting needs.
